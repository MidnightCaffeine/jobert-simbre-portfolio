H.E.L.P: Handheld Emergency Locator for Protection

This project involved the utilization of GPS technology to create a compact emergency button to send real-time location to the police. I successfully developed both the hardware and web application components to ensure efficient sending, recieving and submitting reports for the cases.

The H.E.L.P: Handheld Emergency Locator for Protection system combines Arduino technology with a web-based interface to ensure quick reaction in critical situations. The Arduino board is linked to an actual emergency button that, when pressed, generates an alert. This alarm is sent over the internet to a centralized online system that is accessible to designated responders. The technology sends real-time information, indicating the location of the emergency, allowing for prompt intervention and ensuring the safety of those in danger. Our solution, which seamlessly integrates hardware and web technologies, provides an efficient and dependable way of emergency response in a variety of scenarios.
